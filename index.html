Who are we?

Meow ltd. is an online marketplace helping employers find top talent faster with no agency fees. By personalising recruitment, Meow ltd. gives candidates a voice beyond just a CV, making it easier for employers to match quickly and find the best fit.

Save time

Set your hiring criteria and Meow ltd. are able to share qualifying candidates within hours saving you time and money.

Utilising proprietary technology to search and match the best candidates for your role.

Improve reach

We have integrated with major job boards to improve reach but also with niche job boards, blogs, and content websites.

Leveraging unique reach within key universities and career groups.

Fixed price

Unlike a traditional agency, we offer a fixed price model allowing you to control your hiring costs saving on recruitment fees.

Enabling you to save your business time and other internal costs.
